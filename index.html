Our Goal

Welcome to Vorsy Pizza Online Ordering System! We aim to make pizza ordering simple and convenient for our customers. Order online and get your pizza delivered fast and fresh!

Ordering Process

  1. Open the Vorsy Pizza Homepage.
  2. Navigate to the Order Section.
  3. Fill out the order form with details (customer info, pizza type, quantity, delivery date/time).
  4. System validates the inputs.
  5. Calculate the total price of the order.
  6. Save the order in the system.
  7. View the list of all pizza orders below.

Note: This process ensures quick and efficient pizza ordering for our customers.
